Unreal FPS Game

This game is an FPS where the goal is to survive against a variety of enemies. The main gameplay is dodging projectiles but the player can also kill enemies for score.

This is the first game I worked on in Unreal Engine. It taught me a lot about the workflow for creating a game.

Things that I learnt from this project:

  • Unreal Engine experience
  • Team management
  • Visual scripting
  • Level Design
