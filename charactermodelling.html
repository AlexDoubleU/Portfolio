Character Modelling

Project Type University Project
Duration 1 Month
Software Used Maya

About

This was my first attempt at character modelling, and was for my university unit, modelling for animation. We started off by finding reference images for a model, and cleaned the images on photoshop. I'm proud of my work here because I wouldn't consider myself a modeller; despite this, I dedicated extra time to make sure I made something I was happy with.

Given additional time to work on the model, my focus would be on optimising polygon count, particularly for the hand. Additionally, I would improve the face, especially the mouth as it doesn't fit the reference well and could potentially look strange when animated.

Here is the original reference, and the refined version with lines cleaned and arms adjusted into an A pose, improving its suitability for animation.
