About Me

Hi, I'm Alex! I've always enjoyed playing video games. Over the years I've reached a deep understanding of games which led me into a professional career in esports. Now I'd like to use my understanding of games to create experiences like those that games previously have given to me.

I'm currently studying Games Design at Bournemouth University. My main areas of interest are general game design and level design.
