About Me

Hey there, I'm Alex, a recent Games Design graduate from Bournemouth University, awarded a First Class Honours degree. I love the design process in games, where you create a prototype and iterate on the design to make the game as engaging as you can.

I specialise in game design, with a specific focus on the fundamental aspects of gameplay mechanics and player interactions. My passion for games has developed a curiosity in me about the underlying principles that drive game development. This curiosity fuels my desire to contribute to the development of engaging gaming experiences.

I'm currently working on my own personal projects and will showcase stuff that I'm happy with.
