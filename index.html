About Me

Hello there, I'm Alex. I'm a recent Games Design graduate from Bournemouth University, where I achieved a First Class Honours degree. My primary focus lies in game and systems design.

Video games are a passion for me that has grown into a deep understanding of their inner workings. Even a brief stint in esports contributed to my unique perspective. Now, I aim to apply this deep insight to create captivating experiences for others. I'm here to share my genuine enthusiasm for creating meaningful gaming moments.
